She-Ra and The Princesses Of Power

Character Info

Catra

Catra is one of the main characters in "She-Ra and the Princesses of Power". She is the deuteragonist and a former antagonist. Starting out as a cadet in the Horde, Catra was soon promoted to the rank of Force Captain and eventually served as Hordak's second in command. After the invasion of the Galactic Horde, Catra briefly served as an agent of Horde Prime. After her reform, she became Adora's girlfriend.

Adora

Adora is the main protagonist of She-Ra and the Princesses of Power, the current She-Ra; guardian of Etheria, and co-leader of the Princess Alliance and The Rebellion.

Glimmer

Queen, formerly Princess, Glimmer, is the tritagonist of She-Ra and the Princesses of Power, current Queen of the Bright Moon and co-leader of The Rebellion. She is the daughter of Queen Angella and King Micah and one of Adora's best friends. During the final battle against Horde Prime, she entered into a romantic relationship with her childhood friend Bow. In addition to being queen, she is also one of the Elemental Princesses.

Bow

Bow is a main character from She-Ra and the Princesses of Power. He a companion in the adventures of both Adora and Glimmer.

Gay Stuff

Catradora

Adora is Catra's best friend, and it has been shown that the two once shared an extremely close bond, growing up together as orphans in the Horde. Both Adora and Catra developed romantic feelings for each other but never acted upon them. It takes 5 seasons for these lesbians to finally admit there feelings and end up together.

Bow's Dads

Lance and George, Bow's dads, are pretty minor characters. They have several children together and represent stable, gay relationships that are often missing in media. They do not approve of the rebellion or their son participating
