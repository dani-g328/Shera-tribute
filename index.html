She-Ra and The Princess Of Power

SPOP is a reimaging of the 1980s She-Ra movie.

It heavily features LGBT characters, while updating the story as well

Created by Noelle Stevenson, it aired on Netflix and created 5 seasons.

It ran from 2018 to 2020 and its fan base is pushing for more content.
